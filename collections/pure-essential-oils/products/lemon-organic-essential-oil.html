Lemon Organic Essential Oil

Botanical Name: Citrus limonum

Botanical Family: Rutaceae

Main Constituents:
Limonene: 55 – 70%

Plant Part: Fruit Peel

Origin: Italy

Cultivation Method: USDA Certified Organic

Processing Method: Cold Pressed

﻿Approximate Shelf Life: ﻿1 - 2 years

Aromatic Scent: A strong top note.  Bittersweet, Bright, Cool, Fresh, Fruity, and Lemony.

Blends Well With: Angelica Root, Bergamot, Frankincense frereana, Lime, Orange, Mandarin and Rosalina

Lemon supports the 2nd, 3rd, and 6th chakras.

 

Tisserand & Young have shown that Lemon essential oil is phototoxic and should not be used topically above concentrations of 2% on areas of skin that will be exposed to sunlight in the next 12 hours.  This oil is harmful when oxidized (including risk of sensitization), non-irritating and may be safe during pregnancy. [1]

Avoid old or oxidized Lemon oil.  Store in refrigerator to extend shelf life.

We do not recommend topical use in concentrations above 2%

This oil is safe for children.

This oil has GRAS status.  (Generally regarded as safe)

References

  1. Tisserand & Young Essential Oil Safety, 2ndEdition Churchill Livingstone Elsevier. Edinburg 2014 

Extreme overuse of essential oils can be very dangerous, and even fatal in rare cases(ingestion by children).  Always follow our General Safety Guidelines and dilute your oils.  Some people may have adverse or allergic reactions.  Do not ingest essential oil.  Avoid contact with eyes and sensitive areas.  Keep out of reach of children.
